Color Contrast

Color contrast is not only necessary for blind people but also for the rest of us : in a bright sunlight, contrast is essential !

Use Sufficient contrast ratio : minimum 4.5 but preferred 7 !

Color Meaning

Not anyone perceive color the same way. 8% of males are affected by red-green-blue blindness.

Do not use colors as the only visual indicator of a meaning. For example, keep links underlined.

Tips :

  • If you want to improve readability of links, use the css properties text-underline-offsetand text-decoration-color.
  • Icons and text could be a good way to communicate meaning in addition with colors.
